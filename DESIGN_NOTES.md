# Your Next Place - Design & Content Reference

## Design Analysis

### Color Scheme
- **Primary Brand Color**: Coral/Salmon Red (#04055e or similar)
- **Secondary Color**: Deep Teal/Navy (#1B4D5C or similar)
- **Accent**: Purple/Violet buttons (#7B5BA3 or similar)
- **Background**: White/Light Gray
- **Text**: Dark gray/charcoal

### Layout & Structure
- **Header**: Fixed navigation bar with logo and menu items (HOME, SERVICES, ABOUT, CONTACT)
- **Hero Section**: Coral background with circular image on left, large headline on right
- **Sections**: Alternating layouts with text and images
- **Footer**: Dark teal background with contact info and social links

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold, large sans-serif (appears to be a modern font)
- **Body**: Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Accent Text**: Brand name "YOUR NEXT PLACE" in coral red

### Key Visual Elements
- Circular image frames
- Diagonal/curved section dividers
- Purple rounded button with arrow
- Property images (colorful townhouses, modern interiors, elegant doors)
- Testimonial quote styling

## Content Structure

### Pages
1. **Home** - Hero, why choose us, services overview, testimonial
2. **Services** - Lettings, Sales, Renovation Consultancy
3. **About** - Company story, team bios (Rudi & Umang)
4. **Contact** - Contact form, address, phone, email, social links

### Key Sections on Home
- Hero with headline and CTA
- "Why Choose Us?" with 5 features (Trusted, Peace of Mind, Competitive Rates, Experience, Portfolio)
- Services cards (Lettings, Sales, Renovation)
- Testimonial
- Contact section

## Navigation Structure
- Main nav: HOME, SERVICES, ABOUT, CONTACT
- Footer links: Home, Services, Lettings, Sales, About, Contact, Legal Notice, Privacy Policy
- Social: WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram
